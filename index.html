About Us

At S.D. RenovaWork, we are passionate about transforming spaces with precision, creativity, and craftsmanship. With over 30 years of experience, we have built a reputation for excellence. Based in Drogheda and serving Dublin and the surrounding areas, we specialise in complete renovations, construction, commercial fit-outs, and architectural solutions.

With a strong foundation in engineering and architecture, our team brings technical expertise together with modern design to deliver projects that are not only functional but visually striking. We pride ourselves on offering a snag-free, high-quality finish on every project, from concept and planning through to completion.

Whether you are a homeowner, contractor, or developer, S.D. RenovaWork provides an all-in-one service. Our commitment to excellence, reliability, and customer satisfaction makes us the trusted choice for clients across Leinster.
